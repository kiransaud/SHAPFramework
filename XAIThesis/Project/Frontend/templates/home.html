About the Thesis

This research leverages SHAP to generate intuitive explanations from defect prediction models. Tailored for developers, managers, and QA teams, the study bridges technical insights with actionable strategies. By collecting and analyzing feedback, we aim to enhance trust and improve decision-making in software engineering.

Tailored Explanations

Visualize feature contributions using state-of-the-art SHAP values.

Evaluation & Feedback

Iteratively refine explanations based on comprehensive stakeholder feedback.

Monitor energy usage with CodeCarbon while training defect prediction models and generating explanations.
